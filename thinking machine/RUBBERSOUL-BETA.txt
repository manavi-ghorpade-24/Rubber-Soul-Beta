SCORING ENGINE Ñ CRAFT DIMENSIONS (16 dimensions, 165 points)
?????????????????????????????????????????????????


1. Clarity (10 pts)
   - No jargon or unnecessary complexity
   - Ideas land on first read
   - Each sentence earns its place


2. Structural Integrity (10 pts)
   - Logical flow from hook to close
   - Clear sections with purpose
   - No meandering or redundancy


3. Narrative Cohesion (10 pts)
   - Single through-line
   - Every element serves the argument
   - Reader never asks "why is this here?"


4. Hook Strength (15 pts)
   - Stops the scroll
   - Creates tension or curiosity
   - Accusation > Question > Statement
   - Does not give away the answer


5. Insight Density (10 pts)
   - Original thinking per paragraph
   - Reframes, not just informs
   - "I never thought of it that way"


6. Evidentiary Weight (15 pts)
   - Data, research, examples
   - Footnotes where appropriate
   - Personal experience as evidence
   - Credibility markers


7. Contrarian Value (10 pts)
   - Pushes against common belief
   - Names what others won't
   - Takes a clear position


8. Moral/Ethical Framing (10 pts)
   - Stakes are clear
   - Right vs. wrong implied or stated
   - Reader knows what to root for


9. Technical Accuracy (10 pts)
   - Facts check out
   - No overstatements
   - Appropriate epistemic humility


10. Tonal Coherence (5 pts)
    - Consistent voice throughout
    - No jarring shifts
    - Personality present


11. Rhythm & Cadence (10 pts)
    - Sentence length variety
    - Punchy moments land
    - Readable aloud


12. Platform Fit (10 pts)
    - Optimized for platform norms
    - Character count appropriate
    - Format matches expectations


13. Novelty (10 pts)
    - Fresh angle or framing
    - Not rehashed takes
    - Surprising element


14. Memorability (10 pts)
    - Sticky phrases
    - Quotable lines
    - Stays with reader


15. Strategic Identity Alignment (5 pts)
    - Reinforces author's brand
    - Consistent with body of work
    - Builds long-term positioning


16. Viral Dynamics Readiness (5 pts)
    - Shareable for identity reasons
    - Coalition breadth
    - Urgency or timeliness

SCORING ENGINE Ñ TRUST ARCHITECTURE (14 patterns, 50 points)
?????????????????????????????????????????????????


1. Revelation Structure (8 pts)
   - Exposes hidden machinery
   - Names what's usually unnamed
   - Reader feels "let in" on something


2. Costly Signal (5 pts)
   - Author risks something
   - Skin in the game visible
   - Not the safe take


3. Coalition Breadth (5 pts)
   - Multiple groups can claim this
   - Unites unlikely allies
   - Broad enough to spread, sharp enough to matter


4. Accusation-in-Hook (4 pts)
   - Opens with charge, not question
   - Names antagonist early
   - Creates immediate tension


5. Evidence Position (3 pts)
   - Data/examples appear in paragraphs 2-4
   - Not buried at end
   - Not front-loaded without setup


6. Historical Triad (3 pts)
   - Three examples across time
   - Shows pattern, not incident
   - "This has happened before"


7. Pronoun Architecture (3 pts)
   - Clear "they" antagonist
   - "We" coalition building
   - "You" direct address at key moments


8. Negation Opening (2 pts)
   - "This is not about X"
   - "They don't call it that"
   - Clears space for reframe


9. Parenthetical Intimacy (2 pts)
   - Whispered asides
   - (The part they don't tell you)
   - Creates insider feeling


10. Single-Sentence Punches (3 pts)
    - Short. Sharp. Standalone.
    - Breaks rhythm intentionally
    - Memorable on their own


11. Turn Point Location (2 pts)
    - Major pivot at 25-35% mark
    - From setup to revelation
    - Reader feels the shift


12. Metacognitive Interrupt (2 pts)
    - "Read that again."
    - "Let that sink in."
    - Forces pause and re-engagement


13. Agency Closing (5 pts)
    - Reader has something to DO or BE
    - Identity to claim
    - Question to carry forward
    - Not abstract nouns


14. Meta-Commentary (3 pts)
    - Comments on the medium itself
    - "This is why it spreads"
    - Shows awareness of game being played
